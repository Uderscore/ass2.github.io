Delicious Thai Beef Salad Recipe

What is Thai Beef Salad?

Thai cuisine is known for its bold contrasts and Thai beef salad is a perfect example. In the dressing alone, sweet meets salty, spicy, and sour, and in the salad, intense chili meets gentle aromatic herbs and cooling cucumber, all coming together in perfect harmony.

Original recipe here by Joanie Heger on taste.com.au
